About OSRI Nepal

The research group formed on July 21, 2023, bringing together exceptional individuals from diverse backgrounds. With a diverse team of researchers and a strong commitment to collaboration, we aim to provide opportunities for students of any level to gain valuable research experience, develop their skills, and contribute to meaningful projects.

Also, the research group remains at the forefront of emerging technologies with the integration of principles of science fields (Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology) with Advanced tech (AI, machine learning, programming). Our group encourages collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers from different disciplines to push the boundaries of understanding by conducting cutting-edge research, exploring new ideas, and generating new insights.
